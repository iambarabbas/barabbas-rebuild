Who We Are

We Are Barabbas

Barabbas was a sinner set free because Jesus took his place. That's who we are. We are sinners saved by grace, freed from condemnation because Christ bore our punishment on the cross.

Barabbas Road Church is on mission to make disciple-making disciples through the personal investment of our lives. We are a church that teaches and preaches the whole counsel of God, trusting that God will grow His church through the means He has set forth in His inerrant and infallible Word.

We invite you to come and meet men, women, and children who have been set free from sin because of Jesus. We are the body of Christ. We are Barabbas.

What We Believe

Hear the Truth

Expository preaching through books of the Bible. We believe God's Word is sufficient, and we teach it faithfully.

Live the Truth

Life groups where we apply Scripture together, share life, and grow in community throughout the week.

Defend the Truth

Equipping classes in theology and apologetics, helping believers articulate and defend their faith.
